# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Ohio-Whiskey-Tracker project! Here are some guidelines to help you get started:

## How to Contribute
1. **Fork the repository**: Create a personal copy of the project.
2. **Clone your fork**: Download your copy to your local machine.
3. **Create a new branch**: Develop a new feature or fix an issue in a dedicated branch.
4. **Make your changes**: Implement your feature or fix.
5. **Commit your changes**: Write clear, descriptive commit messages.
6. **Push to your fork**: Upload your changes to your remote repository.
7. **Create a Pull Request**: Submit your changes for review.

## Code of Conduct
Please adhere to the [Code of Conduct](https://example.com/code_of_conduct) while interacting with the community.

## Issues
Feel free to create an issue if you encounter problems or have suggestions for improvements!
